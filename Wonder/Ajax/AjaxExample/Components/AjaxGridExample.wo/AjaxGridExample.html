All The Pretty Columns

Notes

  • Click the batch size to edit with AjaxInPlaceEditor
  • Click the icon in the upper left hand corner to remove all sorting
  • Click a column title to add it to the sort order. Click it again to change sort direction.
  • The small number next to a sorted column indicates its importance in sorting (1 is most important.
  • The small number next to a sorted column indicates sort direction, subscript is descending, superscript is ascending
  • Click and drag anywhere in a column (except the column title) to re-order the columns.
  • The left column is fixed and can't be moved.
  • Click in the cell to left of a row to select that row. Selection is maintained across batches.
  • Indications of selection, sorting, etc. are all fully stylable and can be replaced with images
  • See the documentation for full usage details and a To Do list.
